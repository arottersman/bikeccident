List of Bicycle Lanes in Roadways

This page lists bicycle lanes and paths that are installed in roadways. Get maps of New York City's bike network

Types of Lanes

  • Standard: A standard bike line is located between a vehicle lane and the parking lane.
  • Curbside: Curbside lanes are painted directly next to a curb on the side of a street without parking.
  • Protected Path: Protected paths are painted between the curb and a lane of parked cars or a jersey barrier.
  • Pedestrian Plaza: Some pedestrian plazas have bike lanes painted along their edges.
